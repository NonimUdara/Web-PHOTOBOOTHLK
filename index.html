What is an art?

Art is a form of expression for imaginative or technical skill. It creates a finished result, a thing. Art encompasses a wide range of human activities involving the creation of visual and performance artifacts as well as the expression of the author's imaginative imagination. A work of art is a finished piece of art that is meant to be seen by others.

Types of Art Creations

Painting Arts

Painting is the application of pigments to a support surface that establishes an image, design or decoration. In art the term “painting” describes both the act and the result. Most painting is created with pigment in liquid form and applied with a brush.

Wood Arts

As both an ancient and contemporary art medium, wood has been used to create sculpture, crafts, and functional objects of art. An extensive array of wood types has been used in the creation of artistic objects. Wood finishes have ranged from natural to painted or stained.

Photo Arts

Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating durable images by recording light, either electronically by means of an image sensor, or chemically by means of a light-sensitive material such as photographic film.
